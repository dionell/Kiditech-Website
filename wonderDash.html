Wonder Dash and Dot



GRADE REQUIREMENT:

Grade K – 3


COURSE DESCRIPTION:

In our 10-lesson Wonder Dash and Dot – Level 1 course, children can learn to code while playing, it is a prerequisite course for the level 2 course, which is a preparation course for Wonder League Robotics Competition. During the classes, students are going to use the five apps from tablets one by one to pass a series of challenges that help them get familiar with the fundamental concepts of sequence and algorithm. After that, they will be provided more challenging and interesting mission in terms of math, physics, science, art and music.


LEARNING OUTCOMES:

  • Learn logical programming while playing with the ready-to-go robots Wonder Dash and Dot
  • Team-up together to win the badge for hundreds of fun challenges
  • Coding as young as possible
