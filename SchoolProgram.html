After School Program

KidiTech can provide 8 and 10 week programs at your school. We currently teach at many schools in the bay area.


  • Trained Coding Staff
  • Low student teacher ratio
  • School portal, Online curriculum and coding tools
  • Robotics and IOT Platforms
  • Grades K-12

Contact us for more information on after school programs.



In School Program

KidiTech can provide customized coding programs at your school, as part of your school day. In addition to all the benefits of after school programs, our instructors can partner with teachers and staff at your school to create a custom curriculum.


Contact us for more information on in school programs.
