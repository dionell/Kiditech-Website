About Kiditech

In Kiditech's mighty world, we coach, play and celebrate an innovative technology program: K-12 STEAM. We gather at Kiditech to learn and have fun with Robots Design, 3D Design and Print, IoT with Arduino and Raspberry Pi, Game Design and more. Together, we discover the evolution of technology and have fun with science, engineering, art and math!
