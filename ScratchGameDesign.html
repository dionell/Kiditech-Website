Game Design with Scratch


GRADE REQUIREMENT:

Grade 5+

COURSE DESCRIPTION:

Scratch is a platform that promotes mathematical and computational skills as well as creativity, reasoning and collaborative working. Students will have exposure to computer science, with the help of Scratch, a basis drag-and-drop block programming tool, and learn how to think like a computer scientist in a structured classroom setting. Students will become familiar with every function in Scratch and challenge themselves to their limits in 10 weeks. They will complete several projects using Scratch while applying the techniques they learned during lessons and will learn how they can use Scratch to make animated simulations and stories by cascading different blocks.

LEARNING OUTCOMES:

  • Be familiar with every function in Scratch
  • Obtain mathematical and computational skills
  • Creativity and reasoning
  • Collaborative working
  • Exposure to computer science
  • Logical thinking
