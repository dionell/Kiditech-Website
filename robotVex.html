Demo Robot Vex IQ

GRADE REQUIREMENT:

Grade 3-6

COURSE DESCRIPTION:

Inspire the engineering in your student as they design and build awesome machines with the Vex IQ Supper Kit and RobotC software. Using visual drag-and-drop/Text programming software, a range of sensors, and teamwork, student can build anything from a autonomous car to a claw robot. We get the creativity flowing right away in this hands-on, engaging course. In this class, students experiment and discover engineering basics via trial and error field testing. Rather than just “snapping parts together,” students learn critical thinking skills and modify their robot designs based on varying conditions. The course exposes students to more complex engineering techniques such as pulleys, inclined planes, and gear use. Let’s get building!

HARDWARE and SOFTWARE PLATFORM

Hardware: Vex IQ supper Kit

Software: VexOS and RobotC programming

LEARNING OUTCOMES:

  • Programming concepts
  • Robotics introduction and basics including sensors, pulleys, levers, gears and motors
  • Engineering Concepts, hands-on building and problem solving
  • Collaborative teamwork for multiple level challenge
  • Presentation and pitch
  • Patience and persistence
