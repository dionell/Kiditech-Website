3D design and printing with Tinkercad

GRADE REQUIREMENT:

Grade 3+

COURSE DESCRIPTION:

This is your student’s chance to see how 3D printing is transforming our world, as they learn how to design simple 3D objects and successfully prepare them for 3D printing with online Tinkercad. They can make something from scratch, or import a more intricate model to use as a starting point—imagine it and create it. The best part of all? They’ll get to actually watch it print and take it home. Students will design multiple objects, and take home at least one printed object of their choice. 3D printing is one of the fastest growing fields in technology. From hardware and software engineering to industrial design and manufacturing, 3D printing industry opportunities are at the forefront of an emerging trend toward fast prototyping and rapid product development. Tinkercad is good for beginner, this course will prepare students for more professional 3D design software like autodesk Maya and Blender.

LEARNING OUTCOMES:

  • Set up the 3D printer process
  • Discover best practices for 3D print creation
  • Learn to work in 3D space
  • Design 3D solid models with Autodesk Maya
  • PTroubleshoot 3D prints
  • Creativity
